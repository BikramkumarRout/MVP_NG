Emine Cosar, MD

Surgeon

Dr. Emine Cosar is an obstetrician-gynecologist with more than twenty years of experience. She completed her residency in Obstetrics & Gynecology and fellowship in Minimally Invasive Gynecological Surgery in Turkey. Dr. Cosar is licensed to practice medicine both in Turkey and New York.
