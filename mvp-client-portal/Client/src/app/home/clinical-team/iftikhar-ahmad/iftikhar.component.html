Dr. Iftikhar Ahmad MD

Vascular and Interventional Radiologist

Dr. Iftikhar Ahmad has over 25 years of experience both in academic and private practice. He held the Chair position at department of radiology of University of Pittsburgh Medical Center and was awarded “Fellowship” by the Society of Interventional Radiology in recognition of his teaching and research. Dr. Ahmad has contributed over 20 articles in peer reviewed journals and multiple book chapters.

  • Medical Bachelor and Bachelor of Surgery, University of Peshawar, Pakistan
  • Member of College of Physicians and Surgeons of Pakistan
