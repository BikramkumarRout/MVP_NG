Eli Halpert, MD

Vascular and Interventional Radiologist Medical Director

Dr. Eli Halpert is board-certified Vascular and Interventional Radiologist. He is licensed to practice medicine in NY, NJ, CT, FL, CA, AZ, WA, and others. Dr. Halpert has been awarded the Citation Certificate of the Office of the President, Borough of Brooklyn, and is a Stars Under Forty honoree. He has published in medical and scientific journals and has been an invited speaker at national medical conferences.

Dr. Halpert has focused his efforts and interests on providing community and long-term care patients with the most advanced minimally invasive therapeutic procedures. Specifically, therapeutic interventions for Limb Salvage, Peripheral Arterial Disease, and Venous Insufficiency, as well as Uterine Fibroid Embolization.
