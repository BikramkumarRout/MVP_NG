Abraham D. Knoll, MD, MRB

Vascular and Interventional Radiologist

Dr. Abraham “Abe” Knoll is a board-certified physician specializing in vascular and interventional radiology. He is licensed to practice medicine in New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Florida, and holds a master’s degree in rabbinic studies. Dr. Knoll is the recipient of several prestigious awards including the Roentgen Resident Fellow Research Award from the Radiological Society of North America (RSNA).
