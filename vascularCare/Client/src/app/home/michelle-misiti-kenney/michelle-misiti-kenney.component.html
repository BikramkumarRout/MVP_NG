Biography

Dr. Abe D. Knoll, MD, DABR, is a board- certified radiologist, and fellowship trained endovascular surgeon, with expertise in arterial and venous disease.

Dr. Knoll received his medical degree from New York University School of Medicine. He subsequently completed his Diagnostic Radiology residency at Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons / Harlem Hospital Center in New York, where he also served as Chief Resident. He completed his fellowship in Vascular and Interventional Radiology at New York University School of Medicine / Bellevue in New York.

Dr. Knoll has presented his published research at numerous national and international medical conferences and received the Roentgen Research Award in 2014 from the Radiology Society of North America (RSNA). Dr. Knoll has always had a keen interest in improving the quality of care within the Long Term Care Setting and is a past recipient of the prestigious Citigroup Entrepreneurial Award for his development of software to aid Long Term Care Facilities with diminishing patient falls. Dr. Knoll lives on Long Island with his wife, also an accomplished physician, and their four children.
