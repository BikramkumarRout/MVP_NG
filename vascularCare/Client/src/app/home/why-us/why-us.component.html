WHY US?

Industry-Leading Vascular Care — Wherever You Are.

MVP provides compassionate and evidence-based care to long-term care residents.

Wounds, blood clots, and other vascular ailments are especially pervasive among the vulnerable population in long-term care facilities. Undertreatment or delay of care can quickly lead to avoidable hospitalization and manifest as irreversible disease.

Our highly-trained and skilled vascular clinicians ensure each patient’s vascular disease is managed properly so they can maintain or improve their overall health

Since inception, our mission has

  • Concierge service
  • Superb patient-outcomes
  • Minimal discomfort
  • Effective communication with both clinicians and family

A New Dimension of Bedside Vascular Care.

Specialized

MVP was created for long term care based on understanding the unique set of LTC challenges. All of our operations — including proprietary technology and clinical services — are tailored to the particular needs of the LTC population.

Mobile

We are equipped to perform some treatment on site, reducing the need for patients to travel out of the facility. When out-patient care is necessary, MVP coordinates the transportation and aide who accompanies the patient to and from their appointment

Preventative

In addition to curing existing disease, our interventions prevent disease from developing. We ensure proper vascular function with detailed treatment plans and consistent check ups, thereby reducing the risk of potential life threatening complications
